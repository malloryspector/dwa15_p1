In 2+ sentences describe who you are and what you do.

I'm a testing development analyst with a background in Graphic Design and a love for Web Development. For a few years after graduating from college, I worked as a Graphic Designer and while I loved the problem solving part of design, it wasn't something I could see myself doing long term. I began to learn how to code and was immediately hooked. I currently do a mix of automated and manual testing for a Boston based e-commerce site, working directly with engineers to ensure code that gets pushed to production won't break the site. I am hoping to continue to learn about programming through work, taking classes, and getting involved in the Web Development community.

Why are you taking this course?

While I'm very familiar with front end developemnt languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), I am interested in learning how to make websites more dynamic. I'm interested in transitioning into a Web Development role in the future and am hoping to continue to increase my skill level and knowlege of programming through this class.

What previous experience do you have with programming?

Prior to this class, I've taken the Intro to Web Programming Using JavaScript class through Harvard Extension school, gone to a lot of Girl Develop It meetups, and have made my way through most of codecademy. On a day to day basis, I use PHP to write automated tests.
